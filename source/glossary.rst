Glossary
========================

.. glossary::
	:sorted:

	cash
		The standard currency in Inkopolis. Used for exchange of items between :term:`users <user>`.
	character
		*In terms of written text,* a letter, digit, space, punctuation mark, etc.
	credits
	C.Q. credits
		The form of currency used on the Deepsea Metro. Used for :doc:`admission to tests </overview/minigames>` and making purchases from the :ref:`vending machine`.
	cog
	module
		A unified group of related commands. (ex: :doc:`Profile </commands/profile>` or :doc:`Metro </commands/metro>`)
	user
		You, and anyone else on Discord.
